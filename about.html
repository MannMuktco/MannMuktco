About

MannMukt.co

MannMukt.co is a collective of compassionate Mental Health Professionals committed to enhancing Mental Health and Well-being through Personalised, Non-judgemental and Empathetic Counselling sessions. With the tagline - From Awareness to Advancement, MannMukt.co was founded as an extension of MannMukt, a non-profit organisation that has been destigmatising Mental Health since 2019. While MannMukt has always focused on creating a ripple effect of awareness, MannMukt.co takes this vision a step further by providing professional Therapeutic Care. Our team of Psychologists prioritises offering a safe and healthy space where you can find healing, stability, clarity and personal growth. Join MannMukt.co on this journey toward holistic Mental Wellness!

Behind our

Logo

The Brain in the logo represents mindful approach, expertise, knowledge, evidence-based practices, and professionalism, guided by a strong ethical foundation. The Sunflower represents empathy, compassion, warmth, care, and growth. Cradling the Brain and the Sunflower, the Hands signify support, guidance, trust and protection. Together they converge toward holistic wellbeing where ethics, expertise and empathy blend seamlessly!
